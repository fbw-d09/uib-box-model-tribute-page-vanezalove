KURT COBAIN

Angel without heaven

"The duty of youth is to challenge corruption."
  • 1967 - born in Aberdeen, Washington, Vereinigte Staaten.
  • 1987 - Cobain formed Nirvana with Krist Novoselic and Aaron Burckhard and established it as part of the Seattle music scene that later became known as grunge.
  • 1989 - he write the song "About a Girl", from Nirvana's 1989 debut album Bleach, after spending three hours listening to Meet the Beatles!
  • 1991 - Nirvana found global success with "Smells Like Teen Spirit" from their critically acclaimed second album Nevermind.
  • 1994 - Died (aged 27) in Seattle, Washington, U.S.
  • 2014 - Cobain was posthumously inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. Rolling Stone included Cobain in its lists of the 100 Greatest Songwriters of All Time, 100 Greatest Guitarists, and 100 Greatest Singers of All Time. He was ranked 7th by MTV in the "22 Greatest Voices in Music".
